## FAQ

### Is this only a chatbot?

`No. The product is designed to move a case into the next real step, including reminders, CRM updates, and human handoff.`

### Does it act without control?

`No. Sensitive steps stay behind approval, and operators see verified summaries before handoff.`

### Is this only a demo?

`The public flow uses deterministic fixtures for repeatability, but it mirrors the same workflow shape used by the live runtime.`

### Who is this best for first?

`Visa agencies, relocation agencies, and similar service teams with multilingual intake and document-heavy workflows.`

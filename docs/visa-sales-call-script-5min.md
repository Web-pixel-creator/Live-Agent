# Visa Sales Call Script (5 Minutes)

Use this when the audience wants a practical product walkthrough instead of a short pitch.

## Goal

Explain the product, the pain, the workflow, and the pilot scope in one short call.

## Minute 1. Opening

`Thanks for the time. I'll keep this simple. We built AI Action Desk for visa and relocation teams that still spend too much time on lead qualification, booking, document follow-up, and internal handoff work.`

`The product is designed to help your team move a case forward from first contact to the next real action, instead of just giving chat responses.`

## Minute 2. Problem Framing

`What we usually hear is that the same problems repeat every day: the first client questions are manual, consultation booking turns into back-and-forth, missing documents require repeated reminders, and final updates still have to be copied into CRM or handed to another operator manually.`

`That is where time gets lost, and that is the workflow we focus on first.`

## Minute 3. Product Explanation

`The way AI Action Desk works is straightforward.`

`First, the agent qualifies the lead.`

`Then it helps book the consultation.`

`Then it follows up on missing documents and reminders.`

`After that, it can prepare the CRM update or escalate the case to the right human owner.`

`The important part is that risky steps stay behind approval, and the operator gets a verified summary instead of a raw transcript.`

## Minute 4. Demo Narrative

`In the live demo, we show one case moving through intake, missing-documents follow-up, consultation reminder, CRM handoff, and finally case escalation.`

`So the audience sees one consistent workflow instead of isolated AI tricks.`

`That makes it clear that this is not only a chatbot and not only a browser runner. It is an operator-safe workflow assistant.`

## Minute 5. Pilot Ask

`The best way to start is not to automate everything at once.`

`We would start with one repeatable workflow, one intake team, and one CRM or handoff path.`

`Then we would measure response time, manual work saved, and handoff quality.`

`If that loop works well, the same pattern can expand into more case types and more teams.`

`If that sounds relevant, the next step is a small pilot around one visa or relocation workflow.`

## Objection Handling

### If they say `We already use chat tools`

Say:

`That's fine. The difference here is that this product is designed to move the case into the next action and the next system update, not just generate a reply.`

### If they say `We need human control`

Say:

`That is exactly why the product is operator-safe. Sensitive steps stay behind approval, and every finished path ends with a clear verified summary and handoff.`

### If they say `We do not want a huge rollout`

Say:

`Neither do we. The right first step is a small pilot around one workflow, one team, and a clear before-and-after metric.`

## Best Closing Question

Use this as the final question:

`If we proved this on one workflow, which part of your current process would you want to automate first: intake, follow-up, or handoff?`

# Visa Founder Pitch Script (60 Seconds)

Use this when you need the shortest possible founder pitch.

## Script

`We built AI Action Desk for visa and relocation agencies.`

`Most of these teams still lose time in the same places: qualifying inbound leads, booking consultations, chasing missing documents, and copying updates into CRM or handing difficult cases to a human operator.`

`Our product keeps that whole case flow in one operator-safe workspace. The agent can qualify the lead, keep the case moving with reminders and follow-ups, prepare CRM updates, and escalate the case to the right human owner when needed.`

`The important part is that it is not just chat and not just browser automation. It has approval boundaries, verified summaries, and clean operator handoff built in.`

`So instead of giving teams another chatbot, we give them an AI Action Desk that helps move a real client case from first contact to human handoff.`

## Delivery Notes

Keep the pace calm.

Emphasize these phrases:

1. `visa and relocation agencies`
2. `operator-safe`
3. `not just chat`
4. `move a real client case forward`

## Optional Shorter Version

`AI Action Desk is an operator-safe agent for visa and relocation teams that qualifies leads, books consultations, follows up on documents, and prepares CRM or human handoff actions from one workspace.`

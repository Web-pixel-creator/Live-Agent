# AI Action Desk Client Deck Outline

## Slide 1. Title

**AI Action Desk for Visa and Relocation Teams**

Subtitle:

`Qualify leads, book consultations, chase documents, and prepare handoffs from one operator-safe workspace.`

## Slide 2. Problem

Use 3-4 bullets:

1. teams repeat the same intake questions,
2. consultation booking becomes manual back-and-forth,
3. missing documents create repeated follow-up work,
4. CRM updates and escalations still depend on manual copy-paste.

## Slide 3. Product

Headline:

`One AI workspace for the full case lifecycle`

Show these stages:

1. intake,
2. booking,
3. document follow-up,
4. reminder,
5. CRM handoff,
6. escalation.

## Slide 4. Why It Is Different

Use 4 bullets:

1. not just chat,
2. not just browser automation,
3. operator-safe approval boundaries,
4. verified summaries and clean handoff output.

## Slide 5. Demo Flow

Use this sequence:

1. `Start New Visa Case`
2. `See Intake Summary`
3. `Request Missing Documents`
4. `See Follow-up Summary`
5. `Prepare Consultation Reminder`
6. `See Reminder Summary`
7. `Prepare CRM Update`
8. `See CRM Summary`
9. `Escalate to Specialist`
10. `See Escalation Summary`

## Slide 6. Value To The Team

Use 4 outcome bullets:

1. faster lead qualification,
2. less repetitive operator work,
3. cleaner CRM writeback,
4. clearer escalation to the right human owner.

## Slide 7. Pilot Scope

Propose one pilot:

1. one visa or relocation workflow,
2. one intake team,
3. one CRM or internal handoff path,
4. measure response time, manual work saved, and handoff quality.

## Slide 8. Close

Close with:

`Start with one workflow, one team, and one repeatable case type. Prove the value on intake, follow-up, and handoff before expanding further.`

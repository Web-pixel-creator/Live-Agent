# AI Action Desk Client Deck Copy

Use this document as ready-to-paste copy for Google Slides, Gamma, Canva, or PowerPoint.

The audience is:

1. a visa agency founder,
2. a relocation agency operator,
3. a sales or operations lead,
4. a pilot customer evaluating workflow automation.

Keep the tone practical.

Do not pitch this as a generic AI platform.

Pitch it as `AI Action Desk for visa and relocation teams`.

## Slide 1. Title

### Slide title

`AI Action Desk for Visa and Relocation Teams`

### Slide subtitle

`Qualify leads, book consultations, chase documents, and prepare handoffs from one operator-safe workspace.`

### Speaker notes

`We built AI Action Desk for service teams that deal with multilingual client intake, repeated follow-ups, and document-heavy workflows. The point is not just to answer questions. The point is to move each case forward.`

### Suggested visual

Use one simple case flow:

`Lead -> Booking -> Documents -> CRM -> Human Handoff`

## Slide 2. Problem

### Slide title

`Where teams lose time today`

### Slide bullets

1. `New leads are still qualified manually.`
2. `Consultation booking turns into repetitive back-and-forth.`
3. `Missing documents create repeated follow-up work.`
4. `CRM updates and escalations still depend on copy-paste.`

### Speaker notes

`This is the real pain we are targeting. Most teams do not lose money because they lack a chatbot. They lose money because people repeat the same intake questions, chase the same documents, and manually rewrite the same case details into internal systems.`

### Suggested visual

Use a split slide:

1. left side `Current process`
2. right side `Where time is lost`
